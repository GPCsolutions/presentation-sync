• Presentation Sync for Google Presentation

      Powered by socket.io, heroku and node.js

      Author:Will Ouyang

      Usage 1: Chrome Extension

      Just add Presentation Sync Extension to your Chrome. Open your Presentation and click the button "Sync Present" on the page.

      Usage 2: Bookmarklet

      1. Add the following link to your bookmark by right click or drag it: Presentation_Sync_Gadget. If you can't add it(on ipad for example), you can add it manually by click here or create a bookmark and fill url field with the text copied from this page

      2. Open your google presentation file and click the bookmark.

      3. Anyone else open the same file and cliked the bookmark will keep be synchronised with you.
